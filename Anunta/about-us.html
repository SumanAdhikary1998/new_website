Ananda Mukerji

Founder and Executive Chairman

Ananda Mukerji, the Founder and Chairman of Anunta, previously pioneered as the founding CEO of Firstsource Solutions, a leading global provider of BPO Solutions. Under his leadership, Firstsource achieved a successful listing on Indian stock exchanges and expanded its footprint across India, the US, UK, and the Philippines.

With over three decades of expertise spanning project finance, corporate finance, and investment banking, Ananda played a pivotal role in launching and managing new ventures during his tenure at ICICI Group, India's premier private banking conglomerate.

An alumni of the prestigious Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Calcutta, and the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur, Ananda boasts both a post-graduate diploma in Management and a graduate degree.

Sivakumar Ramamurthy

CEO and Deputy Managing Director

Sivakumar Ramamurthy (Siva) is the CEO and Deputy Managing Director of Anunta, and also holds a seat on its Board. Previously serving as the company's Deputy Managing Director & COO, he has been a driving force behind Anunta's transformative technology adoption and growth since its inception.

Before his time at Anunta, Siva held significant roles at Firstsource, a top business process management provider. His leadership at Anunta focuses on pioneering technology innovations, steering global business operations, and enhancing customer experience. Recognized as a thought leader in the IT sphere, Siva frequently shares his insights at global tech events. His expertise spans a wide range of IT services, especially in areas of virtualization, cloud services, and remote management.

Siva is an alumnus of Madras University, holding a master's in project management and a degree in Physics.

Vinod Jeyachandran

Executive Director and CTO

Vinod serves as the Executive Director and CTO at Anunta, bringing over two decades of expertise in crafting top-tier network management products. Passionate about Holistic Performance Management, Software-Defined Networks, and Network Function Virtualization, Vinod previously held senior roles at Riverbed Technology and was the VP of Engineering at OPNET Technologies.

As a patent holder and recognized authority in performance management, Vinod continues to shape the industry discourse.

He earned his M.S. in Engineering Science from the University of Toledo, US, and a B.S. from Bharathiyar University, India. Vinod operates from Potomac, MD, USA.

Manvinder Sandhu

President – Sales (North America)

Manvinder boasts a 25-year sales trajectory spanning the US, India, and the Middle East, collaborating with giants like Hyundai Byte Systems, Unisys, and Accenture. He pioneered Unisys's ‘Network Enable’ initiative in Bahrain and spearheaded APAC sales for Corliant until its acquisition by Accenture, where he then transitioned as their Sales Director for Technology Consulting in India.

As a testament to his prowess, Manvinder holds the prestigious Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert (CCIE) title.

He's an engineering graduate from Pune University.
