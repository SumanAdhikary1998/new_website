Our DaaS Partner Program 

Scale your business and maximize your revenue potential by offering your customers a custom-built fully managed DaaS solution tailored to their unique business needs

Anunta’s DaaS Partner Program assists MSPs and reseller partners in selling our Desktop as a Service solutions (Packaged DaaS and Enterprise DaaS) to all businesses and making an attractive profit margin.

With Anunta, you can offer end-to-end DaaS design, implementation, and management services to your own customers.

Our Partner Program gives you the freedom to choose from a variety of offerings to create and sell a custom package.

Our unique approach to partnership enables MSPs to successfully deliver virtual desktops and drive digital transformation to their customers with little to no VDI or DaaS experience and no upfront costs.

Our flexible contracts are designed to provide you with total ownership of all your customers.

About Program

About Anunta’s DaaS Partner Program 

  • Empowering Profitable Collaborations

    Anunta’s Desktop as a Service (DaaS) Partner Program empowers MSPs and reseller partners by facilitating the seamless selling of our advanced DaaS solutions—Packaged DaaS and Enterprise DaaS—to diverse businesses, all while ensuring attractive profit margins.

  • Holistic Design, Implementation, and Management

    By partnering with Anunta, you unlock the opportunity to provide comprehensive DaaS design, implementation, and management services to your clientele, enhancing their operational efficiency and digital prowess.

  • Inclusive Approach to Partnership

    Our distinctive partnership approach ensures MSPs can effectively deliver virtual desktops and spearhead digital transformation initiatives for their customers, requiring minimal VDI or DaaS experience and no upfront costs.

  • Flexible and Customizable Offerings

    Our Partner Program grants the flexibility to select from an array of offerings, allowing you to curate and vend custom packages. Our adaptable contracts are meticulously crafted to ensure you maintain unequivocal ownership of your customer relations.
